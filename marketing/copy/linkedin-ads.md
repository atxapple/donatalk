- Bid: **Lead generation** (form submissions)

### Schedule
- **Launch:** Weekdays (Tuesday–Thursday)
- **Time:** 8 AM–6 PM (prospect's local time)
- **Duration:** Ongoing, review weekly

### A/B Testing Roadmap
- **Week 1–2:** Run all three variants, equal budget
- **Week 3–4:** Increase budget on top performer, reduce underperformer
- **Week 5+:** Double down on winner + test new audience segment or seasonal variant

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Core Principles
- **Warm:** Genuine, approachable, human
- **Professional:** Credible, clear, action-oriented
- **Cause-Driven:** Emphasize dual mission (business + giving back)
- **Energetic:** Positive, forward-looking, empowering

### Do's
- ✓ Use "you" and "your" (first-person perspective)
- ✓ Lead with outcome, not feature
- ✓ Emphasize "warm" vs. "cold" contrast
- ✓ Mention "giving back" / "cause" / "non-profit" explicitly
- ✓ Use short, punchy sentences

### Don'ts
- ✗ Don't use jargon ("revenue acceleration," "sales enablement")
- ✗ Don't oversell or use hype (superlatives like "revolutionary")
- ✗ Don't center the platform; center the outcome
- ✗ Don't imply desperation ("struggling," "failing")

## Additional Notes

- **LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms:** Pre-filled with user's LinkedIn profile data (name, email, company, job title). Drastically improves completion rates.
- **Retargeting:** Consider retargeting visitors to donatalk.com/pitch or landing page with Variant C to drive conversions.
- **Seasonal:** Consider "New Year, New Sales Strategy" angle in January; "Give Back Season" angle in November–December.
- **Lookalike Audience:** After gathering 50+ leads, create LinkedIn lookalike audiences (similar to engaged followers, form completers).
