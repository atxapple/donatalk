## Implementation Notes

1. **Ad Group Structure:** Each ad group targets a distinct sales psychology (warm process, ethical values, meeting outcomes).
2. **Headline/Description Rotation:** Google Ads RSA will test all headlines and descriptions against each other. Provide 5–8 headlines and 3–4 descriptions for best optimization.
3. **Character Limits:** All headlines strictly ≤30 characters; all descriptions strictly ≤90 characters.
4. **Negative Keywords:** Prevents spend on irrelevant search terms unrelated to DonaTalk's value prop.
5. **Retargeting Frequency:** 3/day cap prevents ad fatigue; 30-day window captures decision-makers in consideration phase.
6. **Landing Pages:**
   - Search ad visitors (likely fundraisers) → `/pitcher/signup`
   - Retargeting visitors → `/listener/signup` (higher conversion intent)
7. **Performance Tracking:** Use UTM parameters (`utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=rsa_warm_intro`, etc.) to measure conversion rates and ROAS by ad group.
8. **Geographic Targeting:** Limit to US only (English-speaking markets). Exclude non-English-speaking regions if applicable.
