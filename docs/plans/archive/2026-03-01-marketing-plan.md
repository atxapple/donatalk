# DonaTalk Marketing Plan

> Created: 2026-03-01 | Status: Active | Version: 1.0

## Executive Summary

DonaTalk is a two-sided marketplace that turns sales pitches into charitable donations. This plan targets **sales professionals** (Pitchers) as the primary acquisition audience, using a **LinkedIn-first strategy** with a $2,200/month budget to reach **200 signups in 90 days**.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Budget | $2,200/month |
| Primary target | Sales professionals (US nationwide) |
| Current traction | Zero users |
| 90-day goal | 200 signups |
| Primary channel | LinkedIn Ads + Outreach |
| Secondary play | Non-profit partnerships (long-term) |

---

## 1. Positioning & Messaging

### Core Positioning

"Turn cold outreach into warm introductions — by donating to causes your prospects care about."

### For Sales Professionals (Pitchers)

- **Pain:** Cold emails get ignored. Gatekeepers block you. Prospects don't trust you.
- **Solution:** Commit to donate to your prospect's chosen non-profit, and they'll gladly take your meeting.
- **Hook:** "What if every sales call you made also helped a non-profit?"

### For Supporters (Listeners)

- **Pain:** Constantly bombarded with sales pitches with nothing in return.
- **Solution:** Get paid (via donation to your cause) to listen to pitches you're actually interested in.
- **Hook:** "Get pitched. Get paid. For a cause you believe in."

### Competitive Differentiation

| Alternative | DonaTalk Advantage |
|---|---|
| Cold email / LinkedIn DMs | DonaTalk guarantees a donation — the prospect has a reason to say yes |
| Calendly / meeting schedulers | Those are tools, not incentive systems |
| Charity auctions | DonaTalk is recurring and relationship-driven, not one-off |

## 2. Channel Strategy & Budget Allocation

### Monthly Budget: $2,200

| Channel | Monthly Spend | Purpose |
|---------|--------------|---------|
| LinkedIn Ads | $1,000 | Primary acquisition — sponsored content + lead gen forms |
| Google Ads (Search + Retargeting) | $500 | Capture high-intent searches + retarget site visitors |
| LinkedIn Sales Navigator | $100 | Direct outreach to ideal prospects |
| Email Marketing Tool (e.g. Mailchimp) | $50 | Drip campaigns for nurturing signups |
| Content Creation | $300 | Blog posts, LinkedIn carousels, short video scripts |
| Partnership Outreach Tools | $50 | CRM/outreach for non-profit partnerships |
| Reserve / Testing | $200 | A/B test new channels, boost top-performing content |

### LinkedIn Ads — Primary Channel ($1,000/mo)

**Ad Formats:**

1. **Sponsored Content (carousel):** "3 reasons your next sales call should include a donation" — educational, drives to landing page
2. **Lead Gen Forms:** "Join DonaTalk as a Pitcher — get warm meetings by giving back" — captures signups without leaving LinkedIn
3. **Video Ads (15-30s):** Short explainer of how DonaTalk works, featuring a real use case

**Targeting:**

- Job titles: Account Executive, Sales Manager, BDR, SDR, VP of Sales, Business Development
- Industries: SaaS, financial services, real estate, consulting
- Company size: 11-500 employees (SMB/mid-market — they hustle for meetings)
- Seniority: Mid to Senior

**Expected Performance at $1,000/mo:**

- CPC: $6-10 (LinkedIn B2B average)
- Clicks: 100-166/mo
- Conversion rate: 8-12% (landing page to signup)
- Expected signups: 8-20/mo from LinkedIn alone

### Google Ads ($500/mo)

**Search Campaigns ($300):**

- Keywords: "warm introduction sales," "donate for meetings," "ethical sales outreach," "charitable networking platform"
- Long-tail: "how to get meetings with prospects," "sales meeting incentive"

**Retargeting ($200):**

- Pixel on donatalk.com and app.donatalk.com
- Show ads to visitors who didn't sign up
- Expected retargeting conversion: 3-5% of returning visitors

### Direct Outreach via Sales Navigator ($100/mo)

- Identify 50 ideal Pitcher profiles per week
- Send personalized connection requests with value-prop message
- Follow up with case study / testimonial content
- Expected signups: 5-10/mo (2-5% conversion on 200 outreach/mo)

### Email Drip Campaigns ($50/mo)

**Sequence for New Signups (Haven't Added Funds):**

1. Day 0: Welcome + how it works
2. Day 2: "Here's why sales pros are switching to DonaTalk" (social proof)
3. Day 5: "Your first meeting is one click away" (CTA: add funds)
4. Day 10: "3 non-profits your prospects love" (inspire action)

**Sequence for Dormant Users (No Activity in 14 Days):**

1. "We noticed you haven't shared your link yet"
2. "Here's how [name] got 3 meetings in their first week"
3. Final nudge with limited-time incentive

## 3. 90-Day Roadmap

### Month 1 — Foundation (Days 1-30)

**Goal: 50 signups**

**Week 1-2: Setup**

- Set up LinkedIn Ads Manager and create first 3 ad campaigns
- Install LinkedIn Insight Tag on donatalk.com and app.donatalk.com
- Set up Google Ads with search keywords + retargeting pixel
- Create landing page A/B variant focused on sales professionals (headline test)
- Set up email marketing tool with welcome drip sequence
- Write 4 LinkedIn organic posts (founder story, how it works, use case, FAQ)

**Week 3-4: Launch & Learn**

- Launch LinkedIn Ads (start with $30/day, test 3 audiences)
- Launch Google Search campaigns
- Begin Sales Navigator outreach (50 prospects/week)
- Publish first blog post: "Why the Best Sales Reps Are Also the Most Generous"
- Post 2x/week on LinkedIn from founder's personal account
- Track: CPC, CTR, signup rate, cost per acquisition (CPA)

**Key Metric Targets:**

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Signups | 50 |
| LinkedIn ad CPA | <$40 |
| Landing page conversion | >8% |
| Outreach response rate | >10% |

### Month 2 — Optimize & Scale (Days 31-60)

**Goal: 100 cumulative signups, first completed meetings**

**Acquisition:**

- Kill underperforming ad sets, double down on winners
- Scale LinkedIn spend to top-performing audience segments
- Launch Google retargeting for Month 1 visitors
- Increase Sales Navigator outreach to 75 prospects/week
- Launch Listener acquisition: target non-profit employees and CSR managers on LinkedIn

**Activation:**

- Implement email drip for dormant users
- Personal outreach (email/call) to every signed-up Pitcher who hasn't added funds — understand blockers
- Create "how to share your DonaTalk link" tutorial (video or GIF walkthrough)
- Feature first successful meeting as a case study

**Partnerships (Start Seeding):**

- Identify 20 non-profit organizations in popular categories (education, environment, health)
- Send partnership pitch: "Your donors can earn donations for your cause by listening to pitches"
- Target 2-3 partnership conversations started

**Key Metric Targets:**

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Cumulative signups | 100 |
| Fund addition rate | >20% of Pitchers |
| First completed meetings | 3-5 |
| Email open rate | >35% |

### Month 3 — Growth & Social Proof (Days 61-90)

**Goal: 200 cumulative signups, repeatable acquisition engine**

**Acquisition:**

- Optimize all campaigns based on 60 days of data
- Launch lookalike audience campaigns on LinkedIn (based on converter profiles)
- Test new channels: Twitter/X ads ($200 test budget from reserve), Reddit r/sales sponsored post
- Guest post or podcast appearance in 1-2 sales-focused outlets (Sales Hacker, Pavilion blog, SaaStr)

**Social Proof Engine:**

- Collect 5+ testimonials from early users
- Create "DonaTalk in Action" case study: before/after cold outreach vs. DonaTalk intro
- Launch a "Founding Member" badge for first 200 users (exclusivity + retention)
- User-generated content: encourage Pitchers to post about their DonaTalk experience on LinkedIn

**Partnerships:**

- Close first 1-2 non-profit partnerships
- Co-create content with partner non-profits ("Meet the causes on DonaTalk")
- Explore sales training company partnerships (Sandler, Challenger, local sales meetups)

**Referral Program (Soft Launch):**

- "Invite a colleague" — both get a profile boost or featured placement
- Track referral signups separately to measure viral coefficient

**Key Metric Targets:**

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Cumulative signups | 200 |
| Completed meetings | 15-20 |
| Revenue (platform fees) | $500-1,000 |
| Referral signups | 10% of total |
| Non-profit partnerships | 1-2 signed |

## 4. KPIs & Measurement Framework

### Primary KPIs (Weekly Dashboard)

| KPI | Month 1 | Month 2 | Month 3 |
|-----|---------|---------|---------|
| New signups | 50 | 50 | 100 |
| Cumulative signups | 50 | 100 | 200 |
| Cost per acquisition (CPA) | <$50 | <$35 | <$25 |
| Pitchers who added funds | 5 | 15 | 35 |
| Completed meetings | 0 | 5 | 15 |
| Platform revenue | $0 | $150 | $500 |

### Channel-Specific KPIs

**LinkedIn Ads:**

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| CTR | >0.8% | Refresh ad creative, test new headlines |
| CPC | <$10 | Narrow audience, improve relevance score |
| Conversion rate | >8% | Redesign landing page, simplify signup |

**Google Ads:**

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| CTR | >3% | Revise ad copy, add negative keywords |
| CPC | <$5 | Bid on more long-tail keywords |
| Retargeting conversion | >3% | Test different retargeting creatives |

**Outreach (Sales Navigator):**

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Connection accept rate | >30% | Revise intro message |
| Response rate | >10% | A/B test value prop messaging |
| Signup conversion | >3% | Improve follow-up sequence |

### Funnel Tracking

```
Impression → Click → Landing Page → Signup → Add Funds → Share Link → Meeting
   100%      0.8%       100%          10%       25%         60%        30%
```

**Key Conversion Points to Optimize:**

1. **Landing page to Signup (10%):** Simplify form, add social proof, add video explainer
2. **Signup to Add Funds (25%):** Email drip, personal outreach, reduce friction
3. **Share Link to Meeting (30%):** Provide sharing templates, LinkedIn post templates

### Tools for Measurement

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Google Analytics 4 | Site traffic, funnel tracking | Free |
| Google Ads Manager | Ad performance, conversion tracking | Free (already set up) |
| LinkedIn Campaign Manager | LinkedIn ad performance | Free |
| Firestore queries | User signups, fund additions, meetings | Free (built-in) |
| Spreadsheet dashboard | Weekly KPI rollup | Free |

### Weekly Review Cadence

Every Monday:

1. Pull numbers from all channels
2. Calculate CPA by channel
3. Identify top-performing ad/audience
4. Kill anything with CPA > 2x target
5. Reallocate budget to winners
