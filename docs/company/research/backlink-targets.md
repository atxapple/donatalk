# Backlink Target List — where B2B sales pros gather

> Feeds Obj 2 / KR2-3. **This is a research target list only.** Per Charter §7, autonomous posting is
> gated on Board-approved accounts (Backlog #15) — **nothing here is posted.** When posting turns on,
> earned links get logged in `metrics/backlink-ledger.md`, not here.
> Append-friendly. Started 2026-07-10. Sourced (URLs inline).

## Ground rules (read before any future outreach)
- **No link drops.** Every community below has (or effectively enforces) a no-self-promotion norm.
  Value-first participation only; a link earns its place by being the best answer, per Charter §7
  ("unique, non-templated content," "disclose affiliation," "respect no-self-promotion rules").
- **Disclose affiliation** wherever required (founder/builder tag).
- **Supervised ramp:** first 5 public posts logged + Board spot-checked before unattended posting.
- Prioritize **do-follow** or genuine-referral-traffic venues; note nofollow where known.

## 2026-07-11 additions (growth-research pass — sourced)

### Tier 5a — Cause / CSR / social-impact publications (delivers the deferred "specific outlets + fit")
The donation angle is genuinely newsworthy here (unlike for a generic outreach tool), so these are the
venues pure-sales tools **cannot** credibly enter — the defensible edge. Vet DR + editorial quality; lead
with the *charitable-warm-outreach* story, not a product pitch. All have contributor/pitch paths.
| Venue | What it is | Contributor path | Fit for DonaTalk |
|---|---|---|---|
| **TriplePundit** ([triplepundit.com](https://triplepundit.com/about/)) | Solutions journalism on business + social good; **1000+ contributors** | Exec/consultant/journalist contributor program | Strong — "business that also does good" is its whole beat; the Pitcher-funds-a-cause story fits natively |
| **Stanford Social Innovation Review** ([ssir.org](https://ssir.org/about/submission-guidelines)) | High-DA social-innovation journal | Pitch David V. Johnson (guidelines page) | High-authority; frame as a novel philanthropy-access mechanic, not a sales tool |
| **Nonprofit Quarterly** ([nonprofitquarterly.org](https://nonprofitquarterly.org/submissions/)) | Philanthropy / nonprofit sector news | Pitch submissions@npqmag.org | Angle: a new channel that routes B2B budget into cause donations |
| **Greater Good Science Center (UC Berkeley)** ([ggsc.berkeley.edu](https://ggsc.berkeley.edu/get_involved/write_for_us)) | Science of altruism, generosity, trust | "Write for us" | Thematic gold — the *psychology of a costly, authentic signal* ties research to the product thesis |
| **Causeartist** ([causeartist.com](https://www.causeartist.com/)) | Social entrepreneurship / impact brands | Contributor/feature outreach | Founder-story + "business for good" fit |
| **CSR Times Magazine** ([mag.csrtimes.org](https://mag.csrtimes.org/write-for-csr-times/)) | CSR & sustainability magazine | "Write for CSR Times" (article + bio) | Direct CSR fit; vet DR before investing |
| **YFS Magazine** ([yfsmagazine.com](https://yfsmagazine.com/submit-guest-post/)) | Startup/small-business, high DA | Guest-post submission | Broader founder audience; entrepreneurship angle |
> Note: SSIR / NPQ / GGSC are **editorial, high-trust** (harder to place, high value); CSR Times / YFS are
> easier guest-post paths. Prioritize the editorial three for authority once we have a publishable angle.
