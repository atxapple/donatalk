# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

DonaTalk ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our platform:

### 1.1 Account Information

- Name
- Email address
- User type (Pitcher or Listener)

### 1.2 Meeting & Profile Data

- User-provided information such as bios, donation amounts, and availability
- Meeting scheduling preferences and messages

### 1.3 Payment Information

While we do not store payment card details, we collect payment intent metadata via our third-party payment processor (PayPal).

### 1.4 Communication Data

- Emails you send through the platform
- Notifications and confirmations you receive from our system

### 1.5 Usage Data

- IP address, browser type, device info
- Pages visited and actions taken (for analytics and troubleshooting)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Facilitate introductions between Pitchers and Listeners
- Process and verify payments and donations
- Send email confirmations and notifications
- Provide customer support
- Improve our platform and prevent abuse

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data.

We only share your information with:

- **Payment processor (PayPal)** to handle transactions
- **Firebase** for authentication and database services
- **SMTP email services** to send notifications and confirmations

Your profile information (name, pitch/intro, and donation amount) is visible to other users on the platform as part of the matching process between Pitchers and Listeners.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your data:

- As long as your account is active
- As required to fulfill legal or operational obligations (e.g., for donation receipts)

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us (see Section 9).

## 5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your user experience, for example:

- Remembering login state
- Saving form inputs
- Analyzing traffic via Google Tag Manager

## 6. Children's Privacy

DonaTalk is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including:

- HTTPS encryption
- Secure authentication via Firebase
- Access controls for all backend operations

However, no system is 100% secure. We recommend you use strong passwords and log out after use.

## 8. Your Rights

You may:

- Access or update your personal information
- Request deletion of your account
- Object to processing under certain conditions

Please email us at support@donatalk.com for any requests.

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact:

**DonaTalk Support Team**
Email: support@donatalk.com

## 10. Updates to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on donatalk.com. Your continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance.
